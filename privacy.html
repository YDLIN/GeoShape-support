Privacy Policy

GeoShape Explorer
Last updated: June 2, 2026

GeoShape Explorer is designed as an educational geometry app. The app does not collect, sell, share, or track personal data.

Information We Collect

GeoShape Explorer does not collect personal information from users. The app does not require an account, does not request personal contact details, and does not use advertising identifiers.

Local App Data

The app may store a small amount of non-personal data locally on your device to support the learning experience, including:

  • Learning progress, such as recently opened lessons and completed lesson status.
  • Shape browsing history, such as recently explored geometry models.
  • Display preferences, such as default rotation, edge visibility, grid visibility, annotations, face color mode, and face opacity.

This data is stored only on your device using local app storage. It is not sent to us or to any external service by GeoShape Explorer.

Device Permissions

GeoShape Explorer does not request access to sensitive device permissions such as camera, microphone, photos, location, contacts, health data, or tracking permission.

Network Access

GeoShape Explorer does not use network requests for analytics, advertising, user accounts, cloud sync, or remote content in the current version.

Analytics and Advertising

GeoShape Explorer does not use third-party analytics, advertising networks, or cross-app tracking.

Third-Party Services

The app may include third-party code libraries for app functionality and layout. These libraries do not receive personal data from GeoShape Explorer.

Children's Privacy

GeoShape Explorer is an educational app for learning geometry. Because the app does not collect personal information, it does not knowingly collect personal information from children or any other users.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, share, or transfer personal data, because GeoShape Explorer does not collect personal data.

Data Retention

Locally stored learning progress and display preferences remain on your device until you delete or reset the app data, or uninstall the app.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy when the app changes. If future versions add features that collect or process data, this policy will be updated to explain what data is involved and how it is used.

Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through the support information listed on the App Store product page for GeoShape Explorer.
